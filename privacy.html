Privacy Policy for Aurafy

Last updated: 07 Jule 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Aurafy mobile application ("Aurafy", "the app", "we", "us") handles information. Aurafy is developed and operated by Simobr Studio, based in Morocco. By using Aurafy, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. The short version

Aurafy does not collect, store, or transmit your personal information to us. The app works fully offline. Your quiz answers, readings, history, and settings are saved only on your own device and are never sent to any server we control. We have no accounts, no logins, and no backend.

2. Information stored on your device

When you use Aurafy, the following is saved locally on your device only (using your device's local storage):

  • Your quiz answers and reading history
  • Your Stars balance and in-app progress
  • Your app preferences (language, theme, settings)

This information never leaves your device through us. If you uninstall the app or use its "Reset all data" option, this information is deleted.

3. Advertising

Aurafy displays ads provided by Google AdMob, a Google service. To serve ads, Google and its partners may collect and use certain information automatically, such as your device's advertising identifier, general location (derived from IP address), and app interaction data. This is used to show and measure ads, including personalized ads where permitted.

Google's handling of this data is governed by Google's own policies. You can learn more, and control ad personalization, here:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services

You can reset or limit your advertising identifier at any time in your device's settings (Settings → Google → Ads on Android).

4. Children

Aurafy is intended for users aged 14 and older. It is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

5. Data security

Because your data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us, its security depends on your device. We recommend keeping your device protected with a passcode and up-to-date software.

6. Your rights

Since we do not hold your data, you remain fully in control of it on your device. You can delete all app data at any time through the app's "Reset all data" option or by uninstalling the app.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
